SAP Player Engagement
Role
Visual Designer
Overview
SAP Player Engagement is an application that connects players and coaches of a sports team, particularly national teams that may only be physically together for a couple months each year. Player engagement facilitates and encourages communication between player and coach, as well as player and player.
Problem
When it comes to international sports, most teams are only together for brief periods of time during the year. They prepare and compete in international competition (like the World Cup or Olympics), then go back to their normal routines. For these players, it usually means returning to their respective pro teams and leagues. So how can you make sure that when they do come together, that the team is as ready to go as possible? The platform that SAP Player Engagement provides not only helps solidify the bond between players when they are spread across the globe, but more importantly, allows coaches and trainers to provide training and tactical information in the lead up to important competitions.
