VimoFit
+   A personal trainer on your wrist
Overview
VimoFit is an Android app that acts like a personal trainer in the palm of your hand. Most importantly, with an Android Wear watch, VimoFit uses a patented sensor technology to automatically detect and log your workouts. Users can follow custom routines from Coach Kozak of YouTube fame, or perform their own exercises and let the app track their performance.
Role
Lead UX and visual designer
Problem
We came together with one main goal in mind — How can we use new technology to create a new and valuable exercise experience? With the recent introduction of wearable devices, we felt there could be a great opportunity to bring something new and exciting to the fitness world...something that wasn't a simple pedometer or heart-rate monitor. The motion-detecting technology is a differentiating factor that allows people to focus more on their workouts than on logging their progress.
Designing for Android Wear watches definitely presented a new challenge given the extremely limited screen real estate. Because the sensor technology isn't 100% perfect, we had to provide a way for users to confirm the detected exercise and number of reps, as well as add any additional weight used. Each step was separated into its own screen to maximize touch area for selection and input. A clear and direct 'save' interaction concludes the flow.s
